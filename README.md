# BridgeLearn (Brightway Explorer)

BridgeLearn is a gamified career exploration platform designed to help students discover and experiment with different career paths through interactive micro-projects. Students earn points, unlock rewards, and explore real-world career opportunities while building skills.

Access the app here:

# Overview

BridgeLearn helps students:

- Explore careers through hands-on micro-projects
- Earn points and unlock rewards
- Discover new interests and skills
- Track progress across career pathways

This platform is designed to make career exploration accessible, engaging, and low-risk.

# How to Use the App

## 1. Create an Account
   - Click Login
   - Create a new account
   - Complete onboarding

## 2. Select Your Interests
   - During onboarding, you must select three interests to generate your personalized dashboard.

   - Please select: Technology, Art and Design, and Business
   - These selections will generate a curated dashboard experience.

## 3. Explore Your Dashboard
   - Once onboarding is complete, you will land on your custom dashboard, where you can: View your stats, see recommended micro-projects, track your progress, and explore career pathways
   - Example projects you may see: Build a Personal Website, Design a Logo
   - Clicking on a project card will open a detailed project view.

## 4. Complete Micro-Projects
   - Each project includes: Step-by-step instructions, checklist items, estimated completion time, and difficulty level
   - To complete a project: Open the project, follow the step-by-step guide, complete all checklist items, earn points, and unlock rewards

## 5. Explore Careers and Projects
   - Users can also navigate to: Careers Tab to explore different career pathways and Projects Tab to browse all available projects
   - Each career pathway includes: Linked projects, skill-building opportunities, and career insights
   - Each project is also linked back to one or more career pathways.

## 6. Earn Rewards
   - Navigate to the Rewards page to redeem rewards.
   - Rewards available for redemption appear in green.
   - Rewards unlock based on points earned from completed projects.

## 7. Profile Section
   - In the Profile section, users can: View overall progress, track earned points, review completed projects, and log out
